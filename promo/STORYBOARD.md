## Scene Breakdown

1.  **Scene 1: Awakening**
    *   **Visual:** A stylized VRM avatar opens its eyes within the wireframe structure of a Unity scene.
    *   **Action:** The "MCP Bridge" initializes, represented by glowing data streams.
    *   **Audio:** Subtle digital hum rising to a crescendo.

2.  **Scene 2: The Connection**
    *   **Visual:** The avatar manipulates code blocks (Unity & Python) in real-time.
    *   **Action:** The background transitions from wireframe to a lush, photorealistic game world, symbolizing the power of the integration.
    *   **Audio:** Upbeat, futuristic synth track kicks in.
