# Veo 3.1 Video Generation Guide

Since you have a **Gemini Advanced (Pro)** account, you can generate the video clips for the "The Bridge" promo directly.

## How to Generate

1.  Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com).
2.  Ensure you are using the **Gemini Advanced** model.
3.  Use the prompts below. You can upload the storyboard images (located in this directory) as reference images to guide the generation if the interface allows, or simply use the text prompts.

## Scene Prompts

### Scene 1: Awakening
**Prompt:**
> Generate a cinematic video of a stylized 3D anime avatar's eyes fluttering open inside a wireframe digital cube. Soft, ethereal blue lighting. High fidelity, 8k, raytracing. The mood is mysterious and technological.

### Scene 2: The Connection
**Prompt:**
> Generate an action video of a 3D anime avatar manipulating floating holographic code blocks with 'Unity' and 'Python' logos. Dynamic camera angle. Sparks of data energy flying. The background dissolves from a wireframe grid to a photorealistic lush game world.

### Scene 3: Full Sequence (Try this for a longer clip)
**Prompt:**
> Create a video showing a 3D anime avatar in a wireframe digital world. The avatar wakes up, and as it raises its hands, streams of glowing code flow around it, transforming the wireframe world into a realistic forest. Cinematic lighting, high quality.

## Tips
- **Iterate:** If the first result isn't perfect, ask Gemini to "Make it more dramatic" or "Add more blue lighting."
- **Style:** You can append "Style: Cyberpunk, Anime, Unreal Engine 5 render" to refine the look.
- **Veo 3.1:** These prompts are optimized for Veo's understanding of cinematic movement and lighting.
